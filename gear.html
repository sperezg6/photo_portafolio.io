Canon FTb QL

The Canon FTb QL with a 50 mm lens is a classic 35mm single-lens reflex (SLR) camera, renowned for its robustness and quality. Introduced in the early 1970s, it's a manual focus camera with a mechanically-timed focal-plane shutter. Key features include:


Design and Build: The camera has a solid, durable metal body. It's relatively heavy and sturdy, characteristic of the cameras from that era.


Lens: It typically comes with a 50mm lens, which is versatile for a wide range of photography styles. The lens is known for its sharpness and clarity.


Quick Loading (QL) System: The "QL" in its name stands for "Quick Loading," which refers to a special mechanism that makes loading film easier and faster compared to other cameras of its time.


Shutter and Exposure: The FTb QL offers a range of shutter speeds, from 1 second to 1/1000th of a second, and a bulb mode for long exposures. It has a through-the-lens (TTL) metering system for exposure control, which requires a battery.


Viewfinder: The viewfinder is bright and includes a microprism focusing aid, which helps in achieving sharp focus manually.


Compatibility: This camera uses the Canon FD lens mount, making it compatible with a wide range of FD lenses. I'm currently using a 50 mm lens.


Durability: Known for its reliability and durability, it's a popular choice among vintage camera enthusiasts..

Fujifilm X-T30 ii

The Fujifilm X-T30 II is a compact and lightweight mirrorless digital camera, well-regarded for its high image quality and advanced features. As an update to the original X-T30, it offers several improvements and features:


Sensor and Image Quality: The X-T30 II is equipped with a 26.1-megapixel X-Trans CMOS 4 sensor and an X-Processor 4 image processor. This combination delivers high-resolution images with low noise, even in low light conditions. The unique X-Trans sensor pattern reduces moiré and false colors without the need for an optical low-pass filter.


Body and Design: The camera maintains the retro, classic design of its predecessor, with compact dimensions that make it highly portable. It has a solid build, with a mix of metal and high-quality plastic components.


Electronic Viewfinder and Display: It features a high-resolution electronic viewfinder and a tilting 3-inch touchscreen LCD, offering great visibility and convenience for composing shots from various angles.


Autofocus System: The camera boasts an advanced autofocus system with fast and accurate focusing capabilities, including face and eye detection for human and animal subjects.


Video Capabilities: The X-T30 II supports 4K video recording and offers high-quality video features, including F-Log recording and 4:2:2 10-bit output via HDMI.


Film Simulation Modes: A distinctive feature of Fujifilm cameras, the X-T30 II offers various film simulation modes that mimic the look of classic Fujifilm analog film stocks.

Polariod One Step +

The Polaroid OneStep+ is a modern instant camera that blends classic Polaroid camera design with contemporary features. Here are its key features:


Design: The OneStep+ retains the iconic Polaroid camera look with its boxy shape and rainbow stripe. It's designed to be user-friendly, with a simple and intuitive interface.


Lens System: This camera includes two lens settings a standard lens for typical shots and a portrait lens for close-up photography. The portrait lens allows for better quality images when shooting subjects at a close distance.


Bluetooth Connectivity: One of its standout features is Bluetooth connectivity, which allows the camera to connect to the Polaroid Originals app. This app expands the camera's capabilities with additional features like remote trigger, double exposure, light painting, noise trigger, and manual mode.


Manual Mode: Through the app, the OneStep+ offers a manual mode, giving photographers control over aperture, shutter speed, and more. This is particularly appealing for those who want more creative control over their instant photography.


Film Compatibility: The camera uses i-Type film, which is available in both color and black & white. The film produces classic Polaroid-style instant photos with the distinctive white border.


Flash and Light Settings: It includes a powerful built-in flash and a light meter that automatically adjusts the camera’s settings to the environment, ensuring good exposure in a range of lighting conditions.


Battery Life: The OneStep+ features a long-lasting rechargeable battery, making it more convenient and environmentally friendly than older instant cameras that required frequent battery replacements.


Self-Timer Function: It also includes a self-timer feature, making it easier to be part of group shots or take selfies.
