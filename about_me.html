About Me

I am SPG

Hi, I'm Santiago, a 22-year-old photographer from Mexico City. My photography adventure started last year with my first Polaroid camera. I quickly fell in love with the classic feel of vintage photography. My work is all about capturing moments in a style that brings the past to life. Take a look and see the world through my lens, where every photo tells its own timeless story. I'm currently studying a degree in Computer Engineer at the Instituto Teconlógico Autónomo de México, and I'm also a musician. I play the guitar and as I mentioned I recently discover photography and I've loved everyhting of it since then.


Join me on this trip and if you have any question dm me on Instagram.
